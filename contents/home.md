Mingxuan Liu is a second-year PhD student at the [School of Biomedical Engineering](https://bme.tsinghua.edu.cn/), [Tsinghua University](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/), advised by [Prof. Qiyuan Tian](https://bme.tsinghua.edu.cn/info/1118/1490.htm) in the [Birth Lab](https://birthlab.github.io/). He builds **generalizable AI for neuroscience and neuroimaging**, spanning the full pipeline from acquisition to analysis to clinical translation.

His current work centers on **fetal and perinatal brain MRI** — one of the most data-scarce and clinically demanding domains in the field — with annotation-efficient methods designed to transfer broadly. He is now extending this toward **medical foundation models and agentic AI** that researchers and clinicians can actually trust.

**Research interests:** AI for Neuroscience · Trustworthy Medical Foundation Models & Agentic AI · Annotation-Efficient Adaptation
